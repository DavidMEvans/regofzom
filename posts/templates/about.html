About this register

This register is to compile sightings of possible pre-zombies.


At present it is a simple database to list possible sightings, with funding it will be possible to include extra data as requested.


The listing has been kept to the minimum and it is hoped, dependent on the funding to include, gender, country, approximate age, etc, and images if appropriate.


Question - What is a pre-zombie?


Answer

We will have all seen them ie. sitting on a train/bus/in a car staring into space. They are NOT reading, looking out of windows or on a mobile or listing to music.


Reason why the register is important


At any time in the future their life may fall apart, ie. redundancy, divorce, death in the family, not coping with modern day life.


This could result in them turning into a total zombie and be a threat to society.


Hence the need for a register so some form of action may be taken to avert the threat.



No Leprechauns, Unicorns, Fairies or Zombies were injured in the making of this Database
